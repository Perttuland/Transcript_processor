## Design System

### Color Palette

- **Core Neutrals**:
  - Black: `#282828`
  - Light Grey: `#E6E6E6`
  - Mid Grey: `#828282`
  - Dark Grey: `#505050`

- **Accent Colors**:
  - Green: `#576449`
  - Ochre: `#DA9353`
  - Red: `#D04848`

### Typography

- **Primary**: Sharp Sans (clean, geometric, modern)
- **Fallbacks**: Century Gothic, Arial

### Design Principles

- Minimalist, clean japandi layouts


- Clean iconography
- Confident, intelligent, approachable tone
